Thumper's Library

Website Plan & Design Document

Site Name

Thumper's Library

This name was selected because it:

  • Evokes a playful, friendly character (Thumper the rabbit) that appeals to children and families
  • Creates a memorable and whimsical brand identity for a family-focused bookstore
  • Suggests a cozy, inviting space for reading and learning
  • Is easy to remember and spell, making it accessible for all ages
  • Works well for branding across physical and digital spaces with a distinctive mascot

Optional domain: thumperslibrary.com (or .store, .books)

Site Purpose

Thumper's Library website serves as a digital hub for our whimsical, family-friendly bookstore, providing:

  • A curated catalog of children's books, young adult literature, and family reading materials
  • Interactive storytime schedules, author visits, and educational events
  • Store hours, location, and family-friendly amenities information
  • Age-appropriate book recommendations with reading level indicators
  • Resources for parents and teachers including reading guides and activity sheets
  • A contact form for group visit requests, event registration, and book inquiries
  • A platform for young readers to discover the joy of reading through an engaging, playful interface

Target Audience Scenarios

Scenario 1: Parent of Young Reader

Visitor: "I'm looking for age-appropriate chapter books for my 7-year-old who's just starting to read independently. They love animal stories and adventure. Does Thumper's Library have books sorted by reading level or age recommendations?"

Site Answer: The Browse & Discover page will feature filterable book listings by age range, reading level, and themes like "animal stories" or "adventure." Each book will show recommended age ranges and reading difficulty indicators.

Scenario 2: Elementary School Teacher

Visitor: "I want to organize a class field trip to a bookstore that offers educational activities. Does Thumper's Library have group visit options, and are there any special storytime sessions for school groups? Also, do you have teaching resources for popular children's books?"

Site Answer: The Visit & Events page will display group visit information, educational event schedules, and downloadable teacher resources. A contact form specifically for school/group inquiries will be available.

Scenario 3: Grandparent Seeking Gifts

Visitor: "I'm looking for birthday gift ideas for my grandchildren ages 4, 8, and 11. They all love reading but have different interests. Can I filter books by age and interest to find perfect gifts for each child?"

Site Answer: The book catalog will include advanced filtering by age, interest categories (animals, science, fantasy, etc.), and gift recommendations. Staff picks for each age group will be prominently featured.

Typography

Fredoka (Display)

Used for: Main headings, Logo, Promotional text

Example: "Welcome to Thumper's Library!"

This rounded, friendly sans-serif font is playful and highly readable, perfect for engaging younger audiences while maintaining professionalism.

Quicksand (Sans-Serif)

Used for: Subheadings, Navigation, Buttons

Example: "Weekly Storytime Schedule"

A clean, geometric sans-serif with soft edges that pairs well with Fredoka and maintains excellent readability across all devices.

Open Sans (Sans-Serif)

Used for: Body text, descriptions, form labels

Example: "Our carefully curated selection includes award-winning picture books, engaging chapter books for emerging readers, and captivating young adult novels that families can enjoy together."

This highly readable, neutral sans-serif font provides excellent accessibility for longer text passages and ensures clear communication.

Key Interactive Elements:

  • Age Filter Toggle: Quick buttons to filter content by age group
  • Interactive Book Previews: Hover effects on book covers showing age range and quick info
  • Storytime Countdown: Live countdown to next storytime session
  • Reading Challenge Tracker: Visual tracker for young readers' goals

Special Features for Thumper's Library

Character Integration

  • Thumper Mascot: Animated rabbit character appears in headers/footers
  • Character Messages: Thumper provides tips and reading recommendations
  • Interactive Elements: Clicking Thumper reveals fun facts or animations

Family-Friendly Functionality

  • Reading Level Indicators: Clear visual indicators (1-5 stars or color codes)
  • Parent/Teacher Resources: Downloadable activity sheets and discussion guides
  • Wish List Feature: Kids can create and share book wish lists
  • Book Preview Videos: Short videos of staff reading book excerpts

Educational Components

  • Vocabulary Builders: Highlighted "word of the day" from featured books
  • Author Spotlights: Monthly featured author with biography and Q&A
  • Reading Challenges: Seasonal reading challenges with printable trackers
