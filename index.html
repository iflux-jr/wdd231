About Me

My name is Emmanuel Uko, and I am a student graphic designer with a growing interest in web design and visual communication. I enjoy creating clear, functional, and visually appealing designs that effectively communicate ideas.

I am currently developing my skills in typography, layout, and digital design, with a focus on applying design principles to real-world projects. Through my coursework and personal projects, I have gained experience working with design tools and building simple, user-friendly web layouts.

This website was created as part of a design assignment to demonstrate my understanding of structure, visual hierarchy, and basic web design concepts. I am continually learning and refining my skills as I work toward becoming a well-rounded designer.
