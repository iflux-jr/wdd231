NP Membership Details

Free for Non-Profit Organizations

Perfect for non-profit organizations looking to connect with the community.

  • Free membership for qualified non-profits
  • Basic business directory listing
  • Monthly Chamber newsletter
  • Access to public Chamber events
  • Community networking opportunities
  • Non-profit resource library access
  • Volunteer matching services

Note: Must provide proof of 501(c)(3) status

Bronze Membership Details

$100 per year

Ideal for small businesses and startups.

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Enhanced directory listing with logo
  • 10% discount on Chamber events
  • Access to business training workshops
  • Monthly business development webinars
  • Social media shoutouts
  • Referral network access
  • Business mentoring program

Silver Membership Details

$250 per year

Great for growing businesses seeking more visibility.

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • $100 advertising credits for Chamber publications
  • Priority customer support
  • Exclusive networking events
  • 20% discount on Chamber events
  • Featured in monthly member spotlight email
  • Access to premium market research
  • Business consultation sessions (2 per year)
  • Leadership development opportunities

Gold Membership Details

$500 per year

Premium level for established businesses seeking maximum exposure.

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Homepage spotlight advertisement (rotating)
  • Executive networking roundtables
  • Premium business training sessions
  • Sponsorship opportunities at major events
  • 50% discount on Chamber events
  • Featured in Chamber podcast interviews
  • VIP access to annual Business Expo
  • Dedicated account manager
  • Business consultation sessions (6 per year)
  • Media exposure through Chamber press releases
